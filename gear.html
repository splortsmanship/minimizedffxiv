GEAR 101

Gear comes in several different iLvl (item level) brackets over the course of a tier. Higher item level = more health, better substats, etc. Due to restrictions placed on how many pieces of a given type of gear you can get within a given week (resetting every Tuesday morning), you will not be able to get your BiS (Best in Slot) gear right away.
